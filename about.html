About Sparsh

Our Mission

We teach children and young adults about safe touch and boundary issues, while breaking the silence around sexual harassment. Our goal is to empower, protect, and support through education, awareness, and a future safe space for healing.

Our Vision

Empower. Educate. Protect. We aim to educate youth on safe touch, break the stigma around sexual harassment, and create a safe space for healing and support.

What We Do ?

Awareness

Through powerful social media initiatives, storytelling, and community advocacy, we work to break the silence around sexual harassment. Our awareness campaigns aim to challenge stigma.

Workshops

We conduct interactive sessions in schools, colleges, and communities aimed at educating children, teens, and young adults. These workshops focus on building awareness about safe and unsafe touch, personal boundaries, and creating a supportive environment where participants feel empowered to speak up.

Support Network

Our support network offers a safe, anonymous space for individuals to share their experiences and questions. It serves as a platform for those seeking guidance and encouragement.
